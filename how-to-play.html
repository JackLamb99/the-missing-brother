How to Play

Overview

The Missing Brother is a branching-choice detective story set in 1950s London. Make decisions, gather clues, and uncover the truth behind a dangerous conspiracy.

Getting Started

  1. Click New Game on the main menu
  2. Enter your name and choose your pronouns
  3. Read through the story as you progress through the game and select choices to shape your path

Leaving a scene

Throughout the game there are choices to leave a location or scene, but only some can be returned to.

A red return icon indicates that you can't return.

A green return icon indicates that you can return.

Dice Rolls

Some actions use a dice roll to decide the outcome. You must meet or beat a target number.

A blue dice icon indicates a low risk roll, choosing this option is less risky as it has a lower threshold for success, but there are no second chances.

An orange dice icon indicates a high risk roll, choosing this option is more risky as it has a higher threshold for success, but failure offers a second and even third chance

Item required

The tools icon indicates a choice that requires you to have collected an item to have for the outcome to be successful, it's up to you to decide if you've already found it.
